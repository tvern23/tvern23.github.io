Venue & Location


The conference will be held at Cairns Harbourside Hotel in Cairns Australia, https://cairnsharboursidehotel.com.au . Accomodation should be booked directly with the hotel.

There is a delegate rate of $185 AUD per night including breakfast for a standard room, or $220 per night for a premium room. Additional person in room adds +$50 per day (includes breakfast)

Booking with the special accommodation rate can be made via this link


The hotel provides a free shuttle service to the downtown central district. There are also e-Scooters for rent.
